Events Toolkit

The Events Toolkit has been created for event organisers within the IADT community. It provides standards, processes, guidelines, and tools to assist in the planning and management of both small- & large-scale events at IADT. The information is aimed to help you through the initial stages of preparing for your event.

Events at IADT serve as key communication and collaboration platforms, representing the institution's diverse community and creating memorable experiences. It is essential that all events adhere to shared standards to ensure they are both impactful and aligned with IADTâ€™s strategy, values, and sustainability goals.

Organising events often demands creative and flexible problem-solving. The guidelines and checklists in the Events Toolkit will support you in navigating the event management process, helping you plan and deliver high-quality events.


Steps to a succesful event

  • Got an idea? Does it align with IADT core activities and objectives?
  • Aim? Define the objectives, key messages, and the owner.
  • Audience? Identify who will attend.
  • When? Choose the optimal date and time for your audience.
  • Cost? Determine your budget and available resources here.
  • Plan? Create a production plan.
  • Completed the above steps? Ready to go, or, revisit your approach.

How to execute a succesful event.

Plan

Begin the event management process by outlining the format, defining target audiences, identifying resources, setting the schedule and planning initial actions.

Production

Coordination of key logistics, including venue booking, programme planning, sourcing content, managing technical requirements and catering.

Promotion

Ensure effective event marketing and timely engagement communications to reach the right audience.


Registration

Develop a detailed invitation and registration timeline, incorporating the registration software set up, invitations, registrations and confirmation.

Event Day

Execute the event according to the production schedule, covering every stage from set up to dismantling.

Post Event

Take immediate follow up actions to ensure continuity by completing post-event tasks.
